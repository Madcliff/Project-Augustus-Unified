Project Augustus

A CkII Mod.

Download as .zip Download as .tar.gz View on GitHub

This is a Crusader Kings II mod that looks to recreate the experience of dealing with the Byzantine nightmare of Empires and Cultures of the early middle ages. With a strong focus on new mechanics and on cultural and religious expansion, we welcome you to enjoy the fun of dealing with all new bookmarks and new ways to run your empire.
